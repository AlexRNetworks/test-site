A Passion for Purity

The Hearth & Vine is the realization of a lifelong dream for Chef Eleanor Vance. Raised in a family of vintners and farmers, Chef Vance developed an early appreciation for the profound connection between the land and the table. Her culinary philosophy is simple: honor the integrity of the ingredients.

Our name reflects our identity. 'The Hearth' is the heart of our kitchen—a custom-built, wood-fired grill. 'The Vine' represents our deep roots in wine country and a commitment to a cellar that tells a story.

We believe dining should be an experience—a moment to slow down, to savor, and to connect. We invite you to be our guest and share in our story.
